edus2 by asclepius

Emergency Department Ultrasound Simulator

EDUS2(TM) utilizes an RFID scanner disguised as an ultrasound probe. Tags are placed under the skin of a simulation mannequin which corresponds to live recorded ultrasounds scans on patients presenting to the emergency department. When the scanner reads an RFID tag the software plays the video on screen and the students interprets what he or she sees.

Dependencies

Python-mplayer (http://code.google.com/p/python-mplayer/)

MPlayer (www.mplayerhq.hu) - Make sure it has X11 support built in and the codecs for your scans are available.

An Olimex MOD-RFID125 RFID tag scanner.

Some 125 MHz RFID tags.

Install

Run the python script. Check the wiki for full details.
